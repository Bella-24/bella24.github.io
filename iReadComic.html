  • Hikayat 1: Bermula Sebuah Kisah...

    To Yesterday You (Utusan Dari Kelmarin) tells a story of two brother with different personality and ability, to travel from the present to the past. The older brother, Hael, possessed the ability to travel from the present to the past while the younger brother, Kai, have the ability to telepath. Will they be able to work together despite their difference understanding or will they fail? Read to find out more. -Genre: Historical, Comedy, Young Adult, Superpowers (Please read from right to left.)
